# Introduction

Ben, a data scientist by trade, gets a new job working for IBM in Baltimore Maryland.  Ben has never been to Baltimore before but needs to move his family to Baltimore.  Ben's wife is very concerned about Baltimore because of the high crime rates she has heard about on the nightly news.  Ben's family also loves italian food, and wants to identify neighborhoods where he can find establishments to eat.

Ben decides that he will use open source data, geolocation, and clustering to identify neighborhoods in Baltimore that will satisfy his wifes need to live in a safe place, while also indentifying the best places to eat out..
